CONTROVERSIES

The Khan War It all started in 2008 - a spat between two Khans – Shah Rukh and Salman – which is still a favourite with the tabloids. It was at Katrina Kaif‘s birthday bash when the two had a public spat, and they haven’t made up since.

My Name is Khan In 2009, when Shah Rukh was on a trip to the US to attend an event, he was detained at Newyork airport for over an hour. It happened again in 2012, when Khan was invited to address students at Yale University.

Extra-Marital Affair Rumours doing the rounds on streets suggested that Shah Rukh Khan had an affair with co-star Priyanka Chopra. They were both spotted hand-in-hand, gushing to each other at several events, and the there’s the famous incident of Chopra being (supposedly) spotted leaving SRK’s mansion at 3:00 am.

The Kunder Case At Sanjay Dutt‘s Agneepath bash, Shah Rukh slapped Shirish Kunder, allegedly in response to inappropriate comments Shirish had made about Sanjay’s wife, Manyaata.

Banned from Wankhede Shah Rukh Khan got into a brawl with the security personnel present at Mumbai’s Wankhede Stadium, after the IPL match that happened between KKR and Mumbai Indians. SRK accused the officials of mishandling his kids. The guards said they were just following orders.
