  • Only Indian to have a Statue in Grevin Museum, after Gandhiji !!!
  • In 2008, SRK named by Newsweek as one of the 50 most powerful people in the world.
  • He donated all the prize money KKR won in 2014, 15 cr rupees to the childrens hospital in Kolkata nd Mumbai.
  • Khan owns several properties in India and abroad, including a £20 million apartment in London, and a villa on the Palm Jumeirah in Dubai.
  • Shahrukh Khan is scared of riding a horse.
  • The first time he kissed on screen was in the 2012 film, ‘Jab Tak Hai Jaan’.
  • He started as a television actor in ‘Dil Dariya’ (directed by Lekh Tandon) where he almost went unrecognized because he played a turbaned sardar.
  • He received his first Filmfare award for the film ‘Deewana’.
  • His first name "Shahrukh" means "face of the king".
  • His debut film was Dil Aashna Hai but due to the delay in the release of the film Deewana was considered as his debut film.
  • His acting career in Bollywood was launched by his friend Vivek Vaswani and Hema Malini.
  • All time favorite actor of his since his childhood are Megastar Dilip Kumar and Super star Rajesh Khanna.
  • All time favorite actresses are Megastars Mumtaz, Sharmila Tagore, Zeenat Aman,Reena Roy, and Saira Banu.
  • Is a chain cigarette smoker. Smokes at least 2 packs a day.
  • Had recently patched up with his long time arch rival Megastar Salman Khan at an Iftar Party on July 2013.
